VU Talk - LMS Companion

Welcome to VU Talk, the ultimate Chrome extension for Virtual University of Pakistan (VU) students.

This extension helps you stay on track by automatically syncing your academic deadlines (Quizzes, Assignments, GDBs, and Fee challans) from the VU LMS directly to your Google Calendar.

Why use VU Talk?

  • Never miss a deadline with automatic smartphone notifications.
  • Organize your study schedule in a dedicated "VU LMS" calendar.
  • Easy one-click synchronization.
