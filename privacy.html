Privacy Policy for VU Talk

Effective Date: June 25, 2026

1. Introduction

VU Talk ("we," "us," or "our") is a Chrome extension designed to help students of the Virtual University of Pakistan stay organized by syncing their academic activities from the VU LMS (vulms.vu.edu.pk) to Google Calendar. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

2. Information We Access and Collect

VU Talk accesses the following Google user data through OAuth 2.0:

  • Google Account Email (userinfo.email): To identify the connected account within the extension interface.
  • Google Calendar (auth/calendar and auth/calendar.events): To create a secondary calendar named "VU LMS" and to create, update, or delete academic events (quizzes, assignments, GDBs, and fee deadlines) based on your LMS data.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • We use calendar access exclusively to manage academic deadlines.
  • The extension automates the process of adding LMS deadlines to your personal schedule so you never miss a deadline.
  • We do not read, modify, or delete any of your personal calendars or events unrelated to VU Talk.

4. Data Storage and Security

  • Local Processing: All data synchronization and scraping logic runs locally within your browser using service workers.
  • No External Servers: We do not transmit your LMS data or Google account information to any external servers or third parties.
  • Data Retention: Your data remains on your device and within your Google account. We do not store any copies of your personal information.

5. Google API Disclosure

VU Talk's use and transfer of information received from Google APIs to any other app will adhere to Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
